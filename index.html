About

I am a Postdoctoral Researcher in the research focus “Humans and Generative AI: Trust in Co-Creation” at the bidt. I hold a PhD (Dr.phil.) in Political Communication from University of Passau, where I worked as a Researcher in the ERC Consolidator project “The effects of digitalization on Russia’s informational influence abroad” (RUSINFORM). My research focuses on Artificial Intelligence, Political Communication, Algorithmic Bias and computational research methods in Communication Science.
